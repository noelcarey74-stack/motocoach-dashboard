MotoCoach is an educational tool that provides coaching suggestions for closed-circuit motorcycle track days. It does not replace professional instruction or your own judgement as a rider.

2. Safety

  • Advice in the app is general guidance only and may not be suitable for every rider or situation.
  • MotoCoach is not intended for use on public roads.

3. Subscriptions

Access to premium features is provided through an auto-renewing subscription managed by Apple via your Apple ID. You can cancel or change your subscription at any time in your Apple ID settings.

4. No guarantee of performance

We do not guarantee any specific improvement in lap times, riding skill, or performance. Use the app as one of many tools in your learning.
