Why I built MotoCoach

This isn’t some €2,000 data system. I was sick of trying to get things to work properly, so I made my own.

Every track day felt the same – different apps, gadgets that didn’t connect, gear that cost a fortune and still didn’t give me what I actually wanted.

So I built MotoCoach.

MotoCoach is just an iPhone app that gives you lap times and GPS without the hassle.

No sensors. No cables. No complicated setup.

It works for bikes, cars and karts – anything that moves fast on a circuit. As long as you’ve got an iPhone, you can record your laps, see your data, watch it back as soon as you come in, and share it if you want.
